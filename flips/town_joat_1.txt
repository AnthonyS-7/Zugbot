# [color=00ff00] Town JOAT [/color]

**JOAT (Night, 1-Shot per Option, Once per Night, Privately Submitted)** - You have 3 actions: Cop, Doctor, and Vigilante; you can use each option once per game. Use /act [option] [player] to act. [option] should be "investigate", "protect", or "shoot". 

Eliminate all threats to the town, and ensure at least one town-aligned player remains alive.
